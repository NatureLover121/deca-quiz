Which Event Category is For You?

There are two main categories of DECA events: Roleplays and Prepared Events. There are also two additional categories: Corporate Challenges, and Simulation Challenges. This quiz seeks to tell you which of the four (or multiple) may interest you the most.

However, if you have the time and the ambition, it is almost ALWAYS worth it to try multiple options! Personally, in my freshman year, I thought that I would be much better suited to a written event, but my partner wanted to try a roleplay event. We did both and we qualified for ICDC with only our roleplay. Even if you think that roleplays are not for you... if you have the time, I highly encourage you to try!
